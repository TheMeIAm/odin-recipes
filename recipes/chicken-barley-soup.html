Chicken Barley Soup

from The Bais Yaakov Cookbook

The ultimate one pot meal!

In addition to being delicious, this is a perfectly balanced meal served from one pot!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 carrots, diced
  • 3-4 stalks of celery, diced
  • 1 whole parsnip
  • about 1 lb skinless boneless chicken breast
  • 3/4 cup pearl barley, uncooked
  • 1-2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 10-12 cups water

Steps

  1. Place oil in large stock pot over medium heat. Add onion and saute about 5 minutes, until transparent. Add carrots and celery and saute another 5 minutes.
  2. Add parsnip, chicken breast, and barley to pot. Add salt, pepper, and water to cover.
  3. Bring soup to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to medium low. Simmer 4 to 5 hours.
  4. Remove parsnip and shred chicken before serving.
